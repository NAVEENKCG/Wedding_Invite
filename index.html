A Love Story

Written in the Stars

Born in the same city, under the same golden sun that bathes the ancient gopurams of Kanchipuram — they first met at a kolam competition, their chalk lines crossing as fate would have it. Five years, a thousand cups of filter kaapi, and one memorable Pongal later, Arjun placed a ring woven in Kanjivaram silk beside a deepam at the family temple.

Together, they invite you to witness the sacred union of their families, their stars, and their souls — in a celebration as timeless as the temples that watched them grow.
